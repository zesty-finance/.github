# Zesty Finance

Personal finance that's genuinely exciting and genuinely credible.

## What is Zesty Finance?

Zesty Finance is a personal finance platform built for people who take their financial future seriously but refuse to be bored by it. We're solving the core tension in fintech: most tools are either fun but shallow, or powerful but painfully dull. We're building something that's both.

## Why Zesty?

Finance tools shouldn't feel cold, legacy, or compliance-driven. Money should feel like it's working as hard as the person using it. Zesty Finance is designed for the modern money movement — engaged, forward-thinking people who want real control over their finances without the corporate slog.
